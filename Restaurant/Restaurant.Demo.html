Family Legacy, Bold Flavours

Founded by Chef Rohan Mehta, Saffron & Sizzle brings the vibrant streets of Delhi to London. We believe in slow-cooked curries, tandoor perfection, and locally sourced British produce.

Every dish tells a story — from our signature butter chicken to the sizzling lamb chops. We're not just a restaurant; we're a celebration of spice and community.

— Rohan Mehta, Chef & Founder
